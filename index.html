How It Works

Our 3-Stage Retention System

A complete, done-for-you retention engine — built, managed, and optimised specifically for your brand.

Stage 01 · Build

Automated Campaigns

Save time with smart scheduling and personalised email flows. We build your complete retention engine from the ground up — welcome sequences, abandoned cart, post-purchase, win-back, and more — all tailored to your brand and your customers' behaviour.

Stage 02 · Analyse

Real-Time Analytics

Track performance instantly and make data-driven decisions. Our advanced reporting surfaces cohort lift, campaign vs. flow revenue split, revenue per recipient, and deliverability health — so you always know exactly what's working and why.

Stage 03 · Grow

Seamless Integrations

Connect with Klaviyo, Shopify, and your favourite tools in minutes. We coordinate email, SMS, and popup channels into one unified retention system that compounds customer LTV over time — driving predictable, recurring revenue without additional ad spend.
